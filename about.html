About Me

I’m a Human-Computer Interaction Major at the New Jersey Institute of Technology. I am currently a first-semester Freshman, but I already have a certain future in mind. I chose to major in Human-Computer Interaction so that I can pursue a career that involves working with Artificial Intelligence. I will also be pursuing a Masters Degree in order to further prepare me for this. I want to use the knowledge I gain from the psychology and technology classes that my major entails to understand and complete research for Artificial Intelligence. I am fascinated by the way the technology industry is able to create machines that are able to act like humans, as well as cater to their needs. I aspire to work with such machines, furthering their development and capabilities, and fully understanding them.
